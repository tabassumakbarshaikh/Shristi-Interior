Our Expertise

Services We Offer

Every project is handled in-house by our skilled team. No outsourcing, no shortcuts — quality craftsmanship, delivered on time.

Service 01

Carpenter Services

From solid wood joinery to precision-fitted fixtures — our carpenters bring hands-on expertise to every corner of your home. Whether it's a single shelf or a full room fit-out, we handle it with skill and care.

  • Custom wood furniture & shelving
  • Door & window frame fitting
  • Wooden flooring & panelling
  • Repair & restoration of furniture
  • Home renovation carpentry
Service 02

Interior Carpentry

Custom wardrobes, modular kitchen frames, false ceilings, wall panelling — we craft it all in-house. Every piece is measured, built, and installed by our own team for a perfect fit.

  • Custom wardrobes & storage units
  • Modular kitchen framework
  • False ceiling & POP work
  • TV unit & entertainment cabinets
  • Study tables & office furniture
Service 03

Full Interior Decoration

We don't just furnish spaces — we transform them. From concept to completion, our interior decor service covers layout planning, furniture selection, colour coordination, lighting, and final styling.

  • Space planning & layout design
  • Furniture selection & procurement
  • Colour scheme & wallpaper consultation
  • Lighting design & placement
  • Final styling & finishing touches
