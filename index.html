Which to Choose?

Material Comparison

🪵 Commercial Ply
₹1,150 / sqft
Best for: Storage, Shelving, Basic Cabinets
  • Most affordable option
  • Good for dry indoor areas
  • Study tables, shoe racks, bookshelves
  • Not suitable near water/moisture
⭐ Marine Ply
₹1,800 / sqft
Best for: Kitchen, Bathroom, High-Use Furniture
  • 100% waterproof, no swelling
  • IS 710 certified quality
  • Long life — 15+ years easily
  • Modular kitchens, bathroom vanity
🏆 Semi Marine Ply
₹1,550 / sqft
Best for: Wardrobes, Bedroom, Living Room
  • Good moisture resistance
  • Most popular middle-ground choice
  • Wardrobes, TV units, bed frames
  • Not ideal for wet areas

Materials

Wood We Work With

Teak Wood
Most durable hardwood. Naturally water and termite resistant. Ideal for all furniture and outdoor use.
Sheesham (Rosewood)
Beautiful dark-brown grain, strong and long-lasting. Very popular for bedroom and living room furniture.
Mango Wood
Eco-friendly with unique natural patterns. Affordable and great for tables, shelving, and décor.
Pine Wood
Light, clean, and budget-friendly. Works well for Scandinavian and minimal styles.
